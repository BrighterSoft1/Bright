GOVERNMENT APPROVED

The school is approved by the federal Ministry of Education to conduct the following Examinations.

Basic Education Certificate Examination To transit from the ninth year of the basic education class to the senior secondary, the BECE is conducted for candidates in their third year of the Junior Secondary School.

West Africa Examination Council This is the examination taken by candidates in their last stage of Secondary School Education. It qualifies the student for tertiary education. Enrol your child as we keep counting our success.

National Examination Council This is the examination taken by candidates in their last stage of Secondary School Education. It qualifies the student for tertiary education. It is an indigenous Exam.
